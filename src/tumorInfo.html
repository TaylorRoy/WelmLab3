Tumor Sample Order

Requirements

1. You must have a Material Transfer Agreement (MTA) that is less than 5 years old.

2. You must fill out all required fields in order form.

3. You must select at least ONE sample before you can submit order.

To Get a New MTA

If you do not have an MTA you must fill out questionaire.

Then send filled out questionnaire to Diane Bretones: Dian.Bretones@hci.utah.edu

This will start process of generating a new MTA.

It will take a least 2-3 months.

1.) Excel files must be saved to .csv

1.) Must have at least 5 plate reads, and be in groups of 5 (ex. 5, 10, 15, etc.)

1.) Read mush have 6 blanks in column 24.

1.) Drug file must have 50 drugs.

2.) The word "first" must be placed in front of the first desired read value for each plate.

3.) The word "last" must be placed behind the last desired read value for each plate.
